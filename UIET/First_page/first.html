Welcome to ​Unmasking

In the pandemic wearing masks has become compulsory and the task of identifying people became difficult . Identifying people is an important task to track and monitor the crime rate and other important work . Therefore numerous studies performed or developed to unmask masked facial images using the generative adversarial networks . GANs can be deployed for this task in a variety of ways however there is no detailed comparison of the result of these GAN . Architectures used for the removal of masks objects this works aims to remove masks as objects from facial images using a variety of GANs and then compare them based on numerous parameters such as SSIN, PSNR and MSE scores.
