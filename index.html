About us

 What is MAIVRIK?

MAIVRIK stands for ML, AI, Vision & Robotics Incubation cum Knowledge centre. It has been set up at University Institute of Engineering & Technology (UIET) Panjab University, Chandigarh. It has been established to create industry-accademic partnership by encouraging students, faculty and industrial members to push R&D in the aforementioned discplines.

Our Vision

Our Vision is to ensure that lab serves as a launchapad for new ideas/startups along with creating competitive solution for business enterprises in the domain of machine and deep learning.

 Our Objectives

• Promote industry-academia partnerships to foster consultancy and growth and create industry specific accademic programmes.

• Encouraging students & Faculty in conducting R&D in the fields of AI, Deep Learning, Vision and Robotics.

• Creating a center for academic and industrial innovation in research and new product development to attract global interest and investment.

• Promoting Entrepreneurship and Technoprenures amongst students and faculty members, in sync with Start-Up India Scheme.

• Mobility and international cooperation of Students, scholars and faculty through international tie-ups.
